Our Founder and Principal Consultant

Mr. Vivek Joshi

Vivek has over 25 years experience in Sales, Marketing, Human Resources, Learning & Development and Customer Service. He is an accomplished professional who has a rare distinction of Heading Sales & Marketing and Human Resources.

Vivek has a background in Marketing, Law and Psychology. His background and vast corporate leadership experience enables him to understand human behaviour and the importance of human component in business development.

Vivek has an excellent track record of heading Business Divisions for both Sales & Marketing functions; he was also at the helm of entire gamut of Human Resource Management function, including Administration, L&D and Corporate Communications. He was handling Corporate, field as well as both the Manufacturing Facility at Ahmedabad.
Vivek is a Science and Law graduate of Delhi University with Post Graduate qualification in Marketing from Symbiosis. He has been associated with XLRI, a premier management institute, for sharpening the edges of Talent Management.

Vivek has been associated with MNC like Eli Lilly, Otsuka, Schwabe and Indian giants like Ranbaxy, Sun and Wockhardt.

Vivek is a Certified Trainer and Sales Consultant who works with Corporates for ensuring Customer Delight. He is a Trained Lawyer and a Psychotherapist.
